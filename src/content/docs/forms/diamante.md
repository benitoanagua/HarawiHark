# Diamante

**Pattern**: 1-2-3-4-3-2-1 syllables over **7 lines**.

- Diamond-shaped syllabic poem.
- Creates perfect visual symmetry.
- Often contrasts two opposite concepts.

### Example

```
Sun
bright warm
golden rays shine
through morning clouds dancing
light filters down
gentle soft
peace
```

### Spanish Example

```
Mar
azul claro
olas van y vienen
espuma blanca en la orilla
arena tibia
suave brisa
calma
```
