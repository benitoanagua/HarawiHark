# Limerick

**Pattern**: 8-8-5-5-8 syllables over **5 lines**.

- Humorous five-line form with AABBA rhyme scheme.
- Often tells a short, funny story.
- Lines 1, 2, and 5 have the same meter.
- Lines 3 and 4 are shorter and rhyme with each other.

### Example

```
There once was a cat from Peru
Who dreamed of sailing canoe
It jumped in the boat
But couldn't float
And now it just mews 'I can't do!'
```

### Spanish Example

```
Había un gato en España
Que soñaba con ir a montaña
Subió muy alto
Pegó un salto
Y ahora vive en cabaña
```
