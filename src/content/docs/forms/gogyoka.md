# Gogyōka

**Pattern**: 5 **short** lines, **no strict syllable count**.  
Many English writers approximate 5-7-5-7-7 for consistency.

- Modern Japanese form, conversational tone.
- Ends with a strong emotional or philosophical punch.

### Example

```
Stars above the desert
A lone campfire crackles
Stories weave into night
Ancient voices rise
And settle in the sand
```
