# Tanka

**Pattern**: 5-7-5-7-7 syllables over **5 lines**.

- Japanese court poetry; often lyrical or romantic.
- Think of it as a haiku with two extra 7-syllable lines.

### Example

```
Spring rain whispers
Cherry petals drift downward
Pink snow on still water
Your laughter echoes softly
Across the mirrored sky
```
