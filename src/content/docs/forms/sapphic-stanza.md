# Sapphic Stanza (Syllabic)

**Pattern**: 11-11-11-5 syllables over **4 lines**.

- Ancient Greek origin; modern poets often drop strict meter and keep only syllable counts.

### Example

```
The morning hushes all but birds
Their silver syllables ascend
And stitch the sky with fragile words
of light
```
