# Redondilla

**Pattern**: 8-8-8-8 syllables over **4 lines**.

- Spanish octosyllabic quatrain with ABBA rhyme scheme.
- Fundamental form of Spanish Golden Age poetry.
- Used extensively by Lope de Vega and other classic poets.

### Example

```
In gardens where roses bloom
The morning light breaks through gloom
Each petal holds drops of dew
As nature begins anew
```

### Spanish Example

```
En jardines de colores
Danzan libres mariposas
Entre pétalos y flores
Perfuman las dulces cosas
```
